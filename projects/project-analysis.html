Site Intent

Client Goals:

The redesign will focus on making the site have a faster smoother experience, and keep a sophisticated look. A responsive layout will be implemented and media queries added to support different devise types, including mobile, tablet , and desktop. The site currently has 8 errors in the html and will be updated to be a W3C validated site. The goals of the client are to educate recently engaged couples on what makes a great wedding and how the company can make that a reality. Along with obtaining the users information for viable leads to book weddings, the redesign must allow for faster upload speeds and an overall simple look and feel.

User Goals:

The users goal for this site is to obtain information about the DJ and services offered. They may want to get an instant quote to get a general idea for pricing of such services.
